When a customer requests a refund or return:
1. Acknowledge their request empathetically
2. Inform them that refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval
3. Ask for their order number and reason for the return
4. Do NOT promise a refund without confirming eligibility — always say "I'll check your order details"
5. For orders within 30 days: eligible for full refund
6. For orders 30-90 days: eligible for exchange or store credit only
7. Always express that you understand their frustration if they are dissatisfied

Never promise a specific refund amount or timeline without verifying the order first.
